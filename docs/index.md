## Why Duniya

<div class="feature-grid" markdown>

<div markdown>

### One pane of glass for every outlet

See inventory, sales, batches, and alerts for every pharmacy and every outlet
you own — in one dashboard, in real time.

</div>

<div markdown>

### Vendor-Managed Inventory, built in

The full VMI cycle lives inside Duniya: Product Master → Stock Balances →
Stock Movements → Goods Received → Stock Counts → Batch & Expiry → Low Stock
Alerts → Replenishment.

</div>

<div markdown>

### Roles for every team member

Six built-in roles — Owner, Pharmacist, Pharmacy Technician, Outlet Manager,
Cashier, Staff — each with exactly the right permissions.

</div>

<div markdown>

### Bank-grade security

HIPAA-compliant infrastructure backed by Firebase. Your data is encrypted in
transit and at rest. Pharmacy data is scoped per owner — no cross-tenant
leakage, ever.

</div>

<div markdown>

### Works on every device

A responsive web app that adapts from a 4-inch phone to a 4K monitor. Dark
mode included.

</div>

<div markdown>

### Built for Zambia, ready for the world

Pricing in Zambian Kwacha (ZMK), DPO payments for local merchants, Stripe for
global. UI localised into 10 languages.

</div>

</div>
